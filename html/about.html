Angels Orphanage aims at taking care of the all-round development of financially unstable and displaced children. the organisation's goal is to provide education for all the children placed under its care as well as offer vocational studies to those adolescents who wish to pursue a career in their lives. the organisation also focuses on the mental and psychological wellbeing of each and every child that has been placed under its care. the organisation currently houses 55 lesser privileged children and constantly look after their daily needs as well as providing them with the wholesomeness of love, safety and family that every child deserves.

Opening the charitable organisation, was a shared dream, of Sabina Solomon after almost 20 years of volunteer work in Bangalore, Sabina started her own charitable home for the deserted and orphaned children, she provides the children with a new lease of life. It creates in them a sense of belonging and trust. Every child is tended to, right from the toddlers to the adolescents. the organisation prepares the children for life in a disciplined, optimistic and socially acceptable manner. All children are educated in English medium schools which ensure that they are prepared for life beyond the boundaries of their state and country.
